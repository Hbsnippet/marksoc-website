About Us

MarkSoc – The Marketing Society of Jamia Millia Islamia

We're a dynamic, student-driven community dedicated to exploring the ever-evolving world of marketing. At MarkSoc, we believe marketing is more than just selling — it's storytelling, strategy, creativity, and impact.

Founded with the vision of bridging the gap between classroom knowledge and real-world practice, MarkSoc brings together passionate students who are eager to learn, lead, and innovate.

From hosting speaker sessions to running social media campaigns and solving real business problems, we empower our members with both skill and confidence.

Whether you're a marketing enthusiast, an aspiring entrepreneur, or simply curious — MarkSoc is your launchpad to grow, connect, and lead.
